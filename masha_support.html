Masha Support


Each year, approximately 2,40,000 children are born in India with congenital heart disease. Proper paediatric care in these situations is highly expensive and out of reach for the majority of such children, often costing them their lives.
At Masha Art, we have joined hands with the Heart 2 Heart Foundation to fund surgeries of underprivileged children suffering from congenital heart diseases, throughout the world.

At Masha Art, we also familiarize the underprivileged with being able to choose alternate livelihoods through the medium of art. The artistry created by these individuals is then sold and promoted at our events and all proceeds donated to their supported cause/organizations.
